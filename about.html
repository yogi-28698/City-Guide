About Us

History

We started this website as our final year project. But we wanted to help those who go to new towns and they don't have any clue about anything.
They don't know where they can find basic essentials for their lives.So we think why not devote ourselves to help others to find paths.
It's true that going to a new town is always exciting and fun but these difficulties in finding basic needs are hard to tackle alone or if you have family who rely on you.
Therefore, we are here to help those who are in need. Now we want your support and love to grow .

Our motto

Our motto was always to your users first from start. We are always here to help for others to find their desired basic needs. We are bunch of people who
think about these difficulties you face when you move into a new town and took a initiative to solve your problems. We are always here to provide a path
to you when you feel lost. Here at City Guide we just know one and only one thing "A Path In Unknown".

For any problem feel free to contact us.
